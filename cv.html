My commercial work has recently been Scala-based, and previously centred around the Java stack (Spring / Hibernate / SQL) and HTML / JS. However, I'm more than happy to pick up new technologies, and enjoy the challenge.

Recently I took a small sabbitical to have a break, do some reading, learn Clojure and build some prototypes with a friend.
